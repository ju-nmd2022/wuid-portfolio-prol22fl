EXCITING JÖNKÖPING

Being small and cosy town, Jönköping will always remain in your heart as a place where one can make a lot of fun memories. Here are some alternatives on how to spend spare time here.

Fun activies to do

  • The Match Museum (Tändsticksmuseet)
    •

    Visit one of three museums in the whole world that tells the story of the famous match.

  • Prison Island Huskvarna
    •

    Experience a prison adventure where you solve cells and collect points together as a team.

  • Rosenlunds Rosarium
    •

    Take a walk in Rosenlund's rosarium which is scenically located in an old cultural environment. There are lots of beautiful roses here, but also exotic trees and plantings with perennials.

Delightful places to eat

  • Brooklyn Burger
    •

    Brooklyn Burger is a hamburger chain in Sweden.Its basic philosophy is food that is locally produced and homemade.Restaurant grind and cut meat daily to always achieve the highest possible quality.

  • Pescadores
    •

    Warm, brick-lined restaurant with a nautical vibe featuring seafood specialties & classic desserts.
